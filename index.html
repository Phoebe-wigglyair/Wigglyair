Oh, hi friends.

Its little ol' me, Phoebe "Wigglyair" Bentham (she/her) back and more online than ever

In an attempt to get away from platforms controlled by billionaires, I’ve put together this little website to share what’s going on in my life and to document any lovely moments worth archiving. This small nook on the internet is one place you can guarantee will be free from AI, advertising and censorship, absolutely and forever.

I plan on:

  • Maintaining a blog to share silly things of interest, and media that makes me happy.
  • Keeping a log of session notes from the recording studio
  • Occasional album reviews
  • Archiving gig posters and recorded music credits

Hope you have a wonderful time surfing the web like the good old days, maybe consider making a html website of your own…It really isn’t as hard as you’d think it would be <3
