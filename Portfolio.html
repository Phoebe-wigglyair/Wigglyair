Phoebe Bentham (Producer, Recording/Mixing Engineer)

Pronouns -She/her Education -Diploma with Honours in Audio Recording Technologies (OIART) Instagram -@phoebe_wigglyair

Bio

Phoebe is an accomplished Producer and Recording Engineer working closely with the LGBTQIA+ community in Brighton. As a transgender woman herself, Phoebe looks to uplift artists from all marginalised backgrounds so their voices are clear, defined and sounding incredible. Phoebe is known for her experimental recording techniques, bold punchy mixes and her ability of getting incredible performances from her artists. As a musician she is very knowledgeable when it comes to composition and arrangement, and can use her background in music theory to create harmonies and textures to best serve your song.
